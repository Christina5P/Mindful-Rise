My story

I am!

I am a curious person who has lived a rich and varied life so far.
I grew up in a bit dysfunctional family, which I once believed was "normal. Like a lot of people do."
I love my family, but I missed to take care of myself in a proper way.
I lost my father when I was 17, and I thought my own life would be similarly short.
It has always been crucial for me to prove to myself that I am worthy of love.
My mind has constantly been active, seeking to find my best self and deliver it.
Efficiency became my benchmark, as I grew fearful of not having enough time to accomplish all I wanted.
And as a curious person, that's quite a lot!
This drive led me to burn out, leaving me exhausted and disappointed in myself.

Today, having lived at least half my life, I experience a great sense of freedom.
The greatest freedom is finding and loving myself, and enjoying the wealth of experiences I've gathered along the way.

I want you to feel this same freedom and guide you on your path to becoming your best self.
Your best self is, of course, the place where you enjoy the best and feel comfortable with how to get through ups and downs.
Join me on my blog and explore my materials for self-actualization and personal growth.
